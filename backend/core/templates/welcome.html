ABOUT

This application is made in Kenya.
We as the DreamTeam decided to come up with this application, that was conceptualized with the problem of long queues in Vendor Stores,
mostly, Supermarkets and Malls and considering the outbreak of corona, we saw a societal problem that neede tackling...
The fewer the number of people in a queue enables applicability of Social Distancing thus reduction in number of Corona cases.

SERVICES

Our application the Megga-Dalla offers services to our customers at the comfort of their own home no more queing in lines.

Users are able to select the items they want,make an order and pay for it. Once they are done they come to pick it up when it's already packed.
